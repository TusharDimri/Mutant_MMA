About Us

Mutant MMA Academy is a Mixed Martial Arts academy in Dehradun, Uttarakhand. It is a distinguished academy with the largest number of professional fighters in the state being trained in our academy, aspiring fighters from all different states take training in our academy. Apart from training fighters for their professional career, we also train people from scratch with our fitness program designed for people who want to learn MMA but don;t want to go professional. Whether you want to be fit which is very important in today's toxic lifestyle, you want to learn self-defense or you dream of becoming a professional fighter, we have the path traced for you. With one of the best fighters in the country running the academy, we have world class trainers.

With our fighters excelling in different combat sports we provide an environment where the fighters share their valueable experience with all the Mutants. We welcome people of all all age groups and gender. Our vision is to be the warehouse of best fighters across the globe.
